Who We Are
"Experiences for those who'd rather be doing."

LiVD exists for the individuals, communities, and businesses who want to breathe — who trade screens for summits, commutes for currents, and boardrooms for backcountry.

We curate the outdoor world's best experiences: surf sessions at dawn, singletrack descents through old growth, kite runs on open water, chalk-dusted granite faces, powder days that justify the drive. Vetted guides. Real terrain. No pretense.

LiVD is a movement before it's a marketplace. We're building a community of people who know that the best stories don't happen in front of a screen — they happen outside. IRL.
